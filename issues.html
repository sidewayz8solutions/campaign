Neighborhood Infrastructure & Flooding

Broken drainage systems and poor maintenance have caused unnecessary flooding and property damage. We will modernize our infrastructure with urgency.

  • Repair and upgrade drainage systems citywide
  • Enforce quality standards for road and utility work
  • Publish a public, trackable street repair dashboard
  • Coordinate with state/federal resources for major upgrades
