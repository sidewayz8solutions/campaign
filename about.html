About Frank Scurlock

Innovation, Integrity, and Independence for New Orleans

Who is Frank?

A true son of New Orleans, Frank Mials Scurlock brings a lifetime of business innovation and community service to his campaign for mayor. As a successful entrepreneur who has created over 500 jobs across Louisiana, Frank understands what it takes to revitalize our city's economy.

From his family's legacy with Space Walk — the company that brought joy to millions through inflatable entertainment — to his current work renovating facilities in Gert Town for local manufacturing, Frank has always invested in our community's future.

Experience & Focus

  • Job creator and innovator — 20+ businesses incorporated; 500+ retail stores opened
  • Entrepreneur and creator with a record of turning ideas into action
  • Advocate for transparent, efficient city government
  • Committed to repairing streets and infrastructure
  • Focused on public safety, quality of life, and economic growth

Why Frank is Running

New Orleans is in a crisis of leadership and services. Frank is running to restore trust and deliver results. He will marshal resources, cut through bureaucracy, and hold city hall accountable so neighborhoods can thrive.

Priorities

  • Fix the streets and drainage with transparent timelines
  • Clean, safe neighborhoods with responsive city services
  • Root out waste and corruption at City Hall
  • Support small businesses and create opportunities
